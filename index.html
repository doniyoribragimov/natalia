Meet Natalia Neverko

Natalia Neverko is an award-winning top interior designer. She has been transforming spaces in Miami since 2004. Recognized as a leading interior designer in the area, her projects can be seen in numerous high-end oceanfront properties and condos, including locations like Armani, Turnberry, Acqualina, as well as areas such as Sunny Isles, Surfside, Bal Harbour, and Fisher Island.

OUR DESIGN SERVICEs

From opulent residences to visionary commercial ventures, our innovative approach spans across South Florida, New York, Aspen, and Europe.
